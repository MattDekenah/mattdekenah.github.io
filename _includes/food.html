Food

You'll be able to help yourself to whatever you like from dishes that will be brought to your table. Dodge what you don't like and take what you do! We'll make sure there is some variety, including vegetarian and gluten-free options, for you to pick from.

The kitchens at The Eatery on Silvermist Estate are regrettably not strictly Halaal or Kosher. Please let us know if you would prefer a separate meal, or if you have any other dietary requirements and we'll make sure you are catered for.
